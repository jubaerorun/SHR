Dr. Sayed Hafizur Rahman
Professor

Professor Dr. Syed Hafizur Rahman, an expert in water-related climate change challenges, has been active in environmental research since the mid-1990s. His work includes climate change adaptation, water resource management, and innovative water treatment methodologies. As an educator and certified trainer in disaster management and pedagogy, he plays a key role with the Bangladesh Accreditation Council. He has led significant projects, such as a World Bank-funded initiative at Jahangirnagar University, where he directs the Centre of Excellence in Teaching and Learning. As Director of the Institutional Quality Assurance Cell, he champions initiatives to enhance educational standards and institutional effectiveness.
